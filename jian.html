The Jian Sword

A double-edged straight sword with a rich history in China.

Introduction

The Jian is a double-edged straight sword that has been used in China for over 2,500 years. The first Chinese sources mentioning the Jian date back to the 7th century BCE, during the Spring and Autumn period.

Historical Significance

One of the earliest known specimens of the Jian is the Sword of Goujian. Historical one-handed versions of the Jian typically have blades ranging from 45 to 80 centimeters (18 to 31 inches) in length.

Characteristics

An average Jian with a 70-centimeter (28-inch) blade weighs approximately 700 to 900 grams (1.5 to 2 pounds). There are also larger two-handed versions used for training in various Chinese martial arts.
